Our Clubs

Lep Club
Members not only learn about raising a variety of beautiful native moths, but they get to do it. From egg to caterpillar to cocoon to adult and back to egg again, members participate in the lifecycle of Luna, Cecropia, Polyphemus, and many other moths. (Sponsored by Rosedale Federal Savings and Loan!)
Fossil Club
From fossil collecting excursions to lectures by experts in the field, members are connected to people with a passion for exploring the earth’s past. From shark’s teeth on the shores of the Chesapeake to trilobytes in western Maryland, members increase their knowledge of the geology of our state.
Herp Club
The latest addition to our list of clubs, the Herp Club studies the reptiles and amphibians around the state. Each year the season starts with visiting vernal pools in the spring to watch the nuptial dance of the spotted salamander.

Sponsorship

From highly engaging educational programs to a wide variety of lectures, family events, workshops, and outdoor explorations, we offer corporations a variety of ways to enhance the cultural life of Marylanders. Your sponsorship increases knowledge of our natural world and inspires youth to pursue careers in the natural sciences. Contact us to talk about ways your sponsorship can have the greatest impact.

In addition to helping meet the philanthropic goals of foundations and corporations, we offer many opportunities for exposure, including our online presence, in-house lectures, outdoor programs, and our growing list of members and supporters.
